Census for: May 23, 2025

The Philadelphia Department of Prisons (PDP) posts a census of the incarcerated population every day. The census includes a headcount for each facility. In some cases, a facility is responsible for an incarcerated person who is temporarily outside that facility.

In facility - The total number of incarcerated people currently in custody at a PDP facility. These people are physically inside each facility.

Temporarily not in facility - The total number of incarcerated people who are usually in a PDP facility, but are temporarily in another location. This includes four categories: workers, furlough, open ward, and emergency trips.

  • Workers are outside the facility itself, but working on campus.
  • Furloughs are authorized absences from the facility.
  • Open ward individuals have been admitted to a hospital.
  • Emergency trips include any incarcerated people who have been taken to the hospital, but not yet admitted.

PDP facility totals - The total number of incarcerated people that each facility is responsible for. This includes both people in the facility and people who are temporarily not in the facility.

PDP incarcerated people held in other jurisdictions - The total number of incarcerated people whom PDP is responsible for, but are in custody outside of the PDP system.

PDP total population - The grand total of incarcerated people that PDP is responsible for. This includes people who are in PDP facilities, temporarily out of PDP facilities, and being held in other jurisdictions.
